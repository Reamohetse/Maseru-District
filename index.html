About Us

Our mission as Maseru District Hospital is to provide exceptional healthcare services with compassion and dedication. Our vision is to be the leading healthcare provider in the region.

Maseru District Hospital has been serving the community since 1957, it was known as Queen II before until now is refered as Maseru District Hospital, Maseru District Hospital has grown from a small clinic to a large, multi-specialty hospital...

Our mission is to provide high-quality healthcare services with compassion and professionalism...

Meet our leadership team dedicated to guiding the hospital towards excellence...

We are proud to be accredited by Ministry of Health and have received numerous awards for our services...
